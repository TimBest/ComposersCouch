Requests are a way of starting a dialog between a potential client. They come in two types: public and private. Public requests can be seen by any one on the site. They are used when you know when and where you want to play a show and are looking for bands or venues in an area. After posting a public request other users will apply and you and you can easily sort through the applications and chose the best ones. On the other hand private request are a way of putting together a show when you already know where or who you want to play..
