Book better shows by easily comparing artists and managing booking requests.

Public requests

can be seen by anyone on the site. They are a way of saying i am willing to put on a show at this time near this location. Venues can specify how many bands they are looking for. After a public request is posted users of the site can apply then the poster of the request can go through the submissions and choose the best candidates to finalize a show with.

Private Requests

Are a way of asking artist(s) if they want to play a show. They can be sent to anyone on the site to non site users via email. When reviewing a private request the users is given:

  • The option to accept or decline the request
  • An overview of the participants of a request
  • A quick view at events they already have scheduled close to the date of the request
  • A thread to discuss the details of the event
After the details are sorted out a participant can create an event from the request to finalize the show. and when the participants are ready they can choose to make this event viewable .

Availability Feed

A list of artist in the area or passing by on a tour that may be available. Searchable by location, date, and genre.
